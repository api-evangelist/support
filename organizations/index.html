Zendesk Zendesk provides an integrated on-demand helpdesk - customer support portal solution based on the latest Web 2.0 technologies and design philosophies. The product has an elegant, minimalist design implemented in Ruby on Rails and provides seamless integration of the back-end helpdesk SaaS to a companyu2019s online customer-facing web presence, including hosted support email-ticket integration, online forums, RSS and widgets.
